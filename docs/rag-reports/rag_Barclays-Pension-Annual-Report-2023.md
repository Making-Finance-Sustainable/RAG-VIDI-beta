## Summary
Here is a concise summary of the document:

The document is a Statement of Investment Principles (SIP) for the Defined Contribution (DC) section of the Barclays Bank UK Retirement Fund. The SIP outlines the Trustee's responsibilities, investment objectives, and overarching investment policy for the DC section. The primary objective is to provide members with access to a range of different asset classes at a reasonable cost. The Trustee considers the suitability of investments for members and the need for diversification when setting or reviewing the DC investment strategy.

The document also includes an Implementation Statement, which covers the activities undertaken by the Trustee during the year from 1 October 2022 to 30 September 2023. This includes support and advice from independent consultants on the at-retirement framework, Value for Member assessment, and suitability of self-select investment options.

Key metrics and targets include:

* Reviewing the SIP every three years or after material changes to the investment strategy
* Providing an annual Implementation Statement setting out how the investment policies have been implemented

Actions taken by the Trustee include:

* Consulting with Barclays Bank PLC and considering written advice when preparing and revising the SIP
* Assessing the safeguarding of assets that are not held directly within the custody network provided by the independent custodian
