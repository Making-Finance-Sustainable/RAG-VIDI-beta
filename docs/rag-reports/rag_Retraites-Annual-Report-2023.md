## Summary
Retraites Populaires, a Swiss financial institution, has released its 2023 annual report. The report highlights the institution's resilience in the face of challenging market conditions and geopolitical tensions. Despite these challenges, Retraites Populaires achieved a net performance of 4.3% in 2023, with notable contributions from Swiss bonds, developed market equities, and direct real estate investments.

The institution also reported a significant increase in new financing for public entities in the canton of Vaud, totaling CHF 28.7 million. However, this represents an 82% decrease compared to 2022, due to the normalization of interest rates and the cessation of negative-interest loans.

Retraites Populaires has also made progress in its sustainability efforts, with a focus on responsible investments and environmental considerations. The institution aims to continue playing a key role in the Vaud ecosystem and contributing to the well-being of its clients and stakeholders.

Key metrics and targets mentioned in the report include:

* Net performance: 4.3% (2023)

* Decrease in new financing due to interest rate normalization: 82% (2022-2023)
* Allocation to excess reserves: CHF 32 million
* Unique allocation to pensioners: CHF 3.5 million
