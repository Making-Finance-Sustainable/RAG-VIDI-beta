## Summary
The Caisse de pensions de l'Etat de Vaud (CPEV) has released its 2023 annual report. The report highlights the institution's financial performance and progress towards achieving a degree of coverage of at least 80% by 2052, as required by federal legislation.

Key metrics include:

* Total resources for 2023: CHF 998.8 million, an increase of CHF 36.4 million compared to 2022
* Allocation of assets as of December 31, 2023:
	+ Swiss real estate (direct and indirect): 25.7%
	+ Private placements: 5.6%
	+ Mortgage titles: 5.1%
	+ Bonds: 4.8%
	+ Stocks: 4.5%
* Degree of coverage as of December 31, 2023: 69.7%, above the target of 68.4% set by the recapitalization plan
* Evolution of the degree of coverage from 2012 to 2052:
	+ Gradual decline from 72.1% in 2012 to 68.6% in 2023, followed by a recovery to 68.7% in 2024
	+ Imposed ceiling: gradual increase from 72.1% in 2012 to 75% by 2030 and beyond

The report also highlights the institution's commitment to sustainable finance and responsible investment practices.
