## Summary
The State Administration of Foreign Exchange (SAFE) has implemented various measures to enhance office efficiency, streamline processes, and improve risk prevention. The SAFE has bolstered planning, coordination, and oversight to ensure the implementation of decisions and deployments of the CPC Central Committee and State Council. It has also promoted canceling superfluous documents and meetings, improved investigation and research, and enhanced rule of law on confidentiality.

The SAFE has conducted audits and thematic education on studying and implementing Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era. The guidelines for the construction of the legal system for foreign exchange administration in 2024 aim to enhance legislation in key areas, streamline rules and regulations, and promote administration according to law.

In terms of foreign exchange reserves investments, the SAFE has implemented measures to balance facilitation and risk prevention. It has also conducted audits on foreign exchange administration performance across seven provinces and cities, involving USD 5 trillion in cross-border transactions.
