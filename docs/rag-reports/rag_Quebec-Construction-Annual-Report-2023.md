## Summary
The Commission de la construction du Québec (CCQ) has released its annual management report for 2023. The report highlights the organization's achievements and challenges in the past year. Despite a slight decline in activity in the construction industry, the CCQ's revenues remained strong due to the solid level of activities.

Key metrics include:

* Hours worked in the construction industry: over 200 million hours
* Revenues from the Fonds général d'administration (FGA): increased due to the high level of activities
* Returns on investments managed by the Caisse de dépôt et placement du Québec (CDPQ): 7.11% absolute return, with a value-added of 0.51% compared to reference indices

The report also highlights the CCQ's efforts in sustainable development, including the elaboration of a new Plan d'action de développement durable (PADD) aligned with the government's Stratégie gouvernementale de développement durable (SGDD) 2023-2028.
