## Summary
Khazanah Nasional Berhad's 2023 annual review highlights the organization's commitment to advancing Malaysia through strategic investments and community engagement. The report outlines Khazanah's four core pillars: acting as an active corporate player in Malaysia's capital markets, investing in catalytic sectors through Dana Impak, serving as a global investor to facilitate knowledge and investment flows, and focusing on building vibrant communities for Malaysians.

* RM2.2m contributed for disaster relief assistance, impacting 12,370 families

* RM4.3b RAV 1 for long-term developmental investments with the potential to deliver economic impact
* RM122.5b RAV 1 for investments with commercial returns expectations

The report also highlights Khazanah's focus on sustainability, with initiatives such as the SEMARAK framework, an impact assessment tool, and commitments to support entrepreneurs and VC ecosystems.
