## Summary
MN's annual report for 2023 highlights the company's transformation from an integral pension administrator to a fiduciary asset manager for Dutch occupational pension funds. The report outlines MN's strategic objectives, including the implementation of the Business Transformation Plan (BTP) and the transfer of pension administration to PGGM.

Key metrics include:

* MN achieved its operational targets in 2023
* Large investment amounts were made in various projects
* The fiduciary management of MN Vermogensbeheer was strengthened with results from the first phases of the BTP
* The future of pension administration for PMT has a solid foundation with the decision to transfer mandate to PGGM

The report also includes information on:

* Materiality analysis, which identifies four main themes: sustainable business operations, financial developments, asset management, and pension administration
* Governance and risk management
* Carbon footprint measurement and disclosure in accordance with TCFD recommendations
